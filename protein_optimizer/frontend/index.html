ESM-2 guided mutation
A protein language model proposes substitutions most likely to preserve a foldable, stable backbone — far smarter than random point mutations.
02
BioEmu conformational scoring
Each candidate is scored by its log-likelihood ratio (LLR) between two structural states — the quantitative signal the search optimizes.
03
Genetic selection
The top-20 elite are kept and recombined each round, evolving the population toward your target landscape generation after generation.
Genetic algorithm — how the search keeps the top 20
1 · Generate ~N candidates (ESM-2) → 2 · Score each with BioEmu (LLR) → 3 · Keep the top 20 → 4 · Recombine elite → next round ↺
